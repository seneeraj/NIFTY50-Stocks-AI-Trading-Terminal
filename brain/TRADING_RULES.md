# Trading Strategy

## RSI Logic
- RSI < 30 → Strong Buy (Oversold)
- RSI > 70 → Strong Sell (Overbought)

## Trend Logic
- Price > MA20 > MA50 → Bullish Trend → Buy
- Price < MA20 < MA50 → Bearish Trend → Sell

## MACD Logic
- MACD > Signal → Bullish
- MACD < Signal → Bearish

## Volume Logic
- Volume > Avg Volume → Strong Confirmation

## Decision Rules
- Strong Bullish signals → BUY
- Strong Bearish signals → SELL
- Mixed signals → HOLD
